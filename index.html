Hi, I'm Aura.

An AI tool for ethical decision-making

I'm here to help you reflect on your decision-making and uncover new perspectives—so you can make an informed decision while staying true to your values.

Before we begin, take a moment to share what’s on your mind—anything you’re deciding, debating, or feeling unsure about.
Once you’ve shared, I’ll walk you through some ripple effects you might not have noticed—things that could unfold in ways you hadn’t expected.
Then, I’ll offer up some paths you may not have thought of yet. Not to tell you what to do—but to help you see the full picture.
